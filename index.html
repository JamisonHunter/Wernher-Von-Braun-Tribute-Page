The Timeline of Wernher Von Braun

1912: Wernher was born.

1925: Attended boarding school in Ettersburg.

1944: Wernher designed the V2 Rocket during WWII.

1945: Wernher Von Braun surrendered to the United States.

1955: Wernher appears on Disney regarding a manned rocket concept.

1958: NASA is established and Von Braun's Saturn program entered development.

1962: Wernher's Redstone rocket launched first American into space.

1969: Apollo 11 of Von Braun's Saturn program lands first men on the moon.

1977: Wernher Von Braun passed away from pancreatic cancer.
